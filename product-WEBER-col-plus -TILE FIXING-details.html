WEBER col plus TILE FIXING Details

Product information

  • Name: product name
  • price: 1000

Product detail

Tile Fixing (col plus )is a ready mix cement-based high performance polymer “latex” modified tile adhesive for internal and external use. col plus has a strong bonding effect, used for adhering large tiles, or in cases of permanent exposure to high degrees of humidity. It can be used in a damp environment, and is water resistant. col plus is non-sagging and classified. The addition of the new Low Dust feature allows a dust reduction by 90% during pouring and mixing of the product!

The Low Dust effect will lead to:

  • Cleaner working sites
  • Easier and quicker renovation.

CHARACTERISTICS

  • Appearance - Powder
  • Composition - Cement, siliceous sand and additives
  • Composition - Synthetic resins, mineral charges and additives
  • Color - Grey or White
  • Powder Density 1.20 ± 0.05
  • Paste density 1.55 ± 0.05
  • Open Time 25 min. at 20°C Pot Life 2 hrs at 20°C
  • Wetting capability at 30 min 90% Transfer at 20 min 85%
  • Shelf Life 12 months
  • Mixing Ratio 5 to 6 lit. per 25 kg 4 to 4.8 lit. per 20 kg
