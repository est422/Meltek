    Why choose us

    Meltek supplies different types of Aquapa Brand pipes and fittings with a high quality German standard, made in Turkey. Our main products are

    • PPR-C Pipe and Fitting.

      • Our product specifications are as follows

    • PPRC PIPES AND FITTING: Pressure nominal(PN) 10 to PN 25 bar with diameter from 20mm to 63m and all type of PPRC fittings

    • PEX AL PEX PIPES AND FITTINGS: PEX-AL-PEX pipe is a PEX pipe with an aluminum layer sandwiched between an inner and outer layer of Cross-Linked Polyethylene (PEX)

    • HDPE Pipe and Fitting

      • (Atlas fittings from UAE) HDPE PIPES AND FITTINGS: PN 6 to 20 bar, diameter from 20mm to 630mm and with all compression and buttweld type fittings.
