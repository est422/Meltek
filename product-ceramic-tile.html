PORCELAIN TILE

Porcelain ceramic tile is a type of ceramic tile known for its durability and versatility. It is made from a mixture of fine clay and other natural materials, which are then fired at extremely high temperatures. The high firing process creates a dense and hardwearing tile that is highly resistant to water, stains, and scratches.

Ceramic floor tile is a type of tile specifically designed for use on floors. It is made from clay and other natural materials, similar to porcelain ceramic tiles. However, there are some differences between the two.

WALL TILE

Ceramic Wall Tile is a type of tile specifically designed for installation on walls. Like ceramic floor tiles, it is made from clay and other natural materials. However, there are some differences in the characteristics and properties of wall tiles compared to floor tiles.
