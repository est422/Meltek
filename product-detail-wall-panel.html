Product information

  • Name: Wall Panel Details

Product detail

PVC panel walls are a type of wall covering made from polyvinyl chloride (PVC) material. They are designed to mimic the look of traditional wall materials such as wood, stone, or brick, walls panel come in a variety of colors to suit different interior design styles and can be installed quickly and easily using Silicones.


ADVANTAGE

High strength Surface is highly durable Resistant to scratches, mold and moisture. Easy installation and maintenance Great alternative to metal or wood.
